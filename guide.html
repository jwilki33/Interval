      How to Run a Session

      1. Set your goal. Log one concrete thing above before opening the Tracker.
      2. Start. Press Start Session — the stability curve begins recording immediately.
      3. Flag distractions. Tap Friction the moment something pulls you away, not after.
      4. End and review. Press End Session to see your focus score and time breakdown.
